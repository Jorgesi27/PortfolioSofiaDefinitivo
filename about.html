About Me

I'm a passionate designer from Portugal, graduated in Design from Universidade de Trás-os-Montes e Alto Douro. I focus on creating modern, elegant, and functional visual experiences.

I have a solid background in graphic design, visual identity, typography, and art direction. I'm deeply inspired by detail, visual harmony, and clear communication.

I work with dedication and sensitivity to transform ideas into impactful and soulful visual projects.
